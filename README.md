# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data of a coffee shop to extract actionable insights that help improve business performance. The analysis is performed using Excel and visualized through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Objective
The main objective of this project is to:
- Analyze sales patterns
- Identify peak hours and days
- Understand customer behavior
- Evaluate product performance
- Provide data-driven business recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Sales:** $6,98,812  
- **Total Footfall:** 1,49,116  
- **Average Bill per Person:** ₹4.69  
- **Average Orders per Person:** 1.44  

---

## 📈 Business Questions Answered
- How do sales vary by day of the week and hour of the day?
- Are there any peak times for sales activity?
- What is the total sales revenue for each month?
- How do sales vary across different store locations?
- What is the average order value per customer?
- Which products are the best-selling in terms of revenue?

---

## 📉 Insights & Analysis

### ⏰ Sales by Time
- Peak sales occur between **8 AM – 10 AM**
- Sales decline after 11 AM
- Lowest performance in evening hours

👉 **Insight:** Morning drives maximum revenue

---

### 📅 Sales by Day
- Highest sales on **Thursday & Friday**
- Lowest sales on **Sunday**

👉 **Insight:** Weekdays outperform weekends

---

### 🏪 Store Performance
- **Hell’s Kitchen:** Highest sales and footfall
- **Astoria:** Moderate performance
- **Lower Manhattan:** Needs improvement

👉 **Insight:** Location plays a key role in sales performance

---

### 🛍️ Top Products
- Barista Espresso (Top performer)
- Brewed Chai Tea
- Hot Chocolate
- Gourmet Brewed Coffee

👉 **Insight:** Coffee-based drinks dominate revenue

---

### 📦 Category Contribution
- Coffee: **39%**
- Bakery: **28%**
- Drinking Chocolate: **12%**

👉 **Insight:** Coffee + Bakery = Major revenue drivers

---

### 🥤 Order Size Distribution
- Regular: 31%
- Large: 30%
- Not Defined: 30%
- Small: 9%

👉 **Insight:** Customers prefer Regular and Large sizes

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

### 1. Increase Revenue
- Introduce combo offers (Coffee + Bakery)
- Upsell large-size drinks

### 2. Improve Evening Sales
- Offer discounts during low hours
- Introduce snacks and combos

### 3. Optimize Store Performance
- Apply best practices from top-performing store
- Improve marketing for low-performing locations

### 4. Product Strategy
- Promote top-selling products
- Bundle slow-moving items

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

---

## 📌 Conclusion
The business operates on a **high-volume, low-value model**, with strong performance in the morning and reliance on coffee products. By improving upselling strategies, evening sales, and store-level optimization, significant growth opportunities can be unlocked.
